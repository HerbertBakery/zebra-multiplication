Rules

  1. Player 1 goes first and is automatically White.
  2. Player 2 is automatically Black.
  3. A player can temporarily switch to place the other color on their turn.
  4. That temporary switch does not carry forward to the next turn.
  5. When the board is full, do not reveal the score right away.
  6. Students work through the score lab.
  7. Find each separate connected group.
  8. Count the size of each group.
  9. Multiply the group sizes one step at a time.
  10. After both player scores are found, compare them to find the winner.

Connected means touching by edges, not just corners.
